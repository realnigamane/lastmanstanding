⚠️ Estimate only — not tax advice

This tool logs every deposit and withdrawal with the crypto market price at the moment it happened, and estimates a tax figure for planning. It is not tax advice and not a filing number. As a US citizen you owe US tax on worldwide income even through a zero-tax foreign company (rules like GILTI, Subpart F, and self-employment tax apply). Operating expenses, cost basis, deductions, and your real bracket will change the number. Give these records and figures to a licensed CPA.
