🎮 How the game works

Climb the rising platforms — move left/right and time your jumps precisely. The floor keeps rising, and balls drop in after a warning — but every ball is dodgeable and every platform holdable. It's pure skill, no luck: out-survive everyone and the last duck standing wins. Start with free Practice to sharpen up.

🏆 Entry fees & prizes

Hit Cash Tournament and choose an entry fee: 10, 50, or 100 credits (1 credit = $1). All entries form the prize pool, and the most skilled player — the last duck standing — takes it, minus a 10% fee. In a full 10-player tournament that's a win of up to 90 / 450 / 900 for those entry fees. Practice is always free.

💰 Deposits & cashing out

Add credits with Bitcoin or Litecoin using the Deposit box below. Cashing out is automatic — enter your BTC/LTC address and amount under Withdraw and it's sent straight to your wallet. Minimum cash-out is 10 credits.
