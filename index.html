🎮 How the game works

Climb the rising platforms — jump left and right to keep moving up. If the floor at the bottom catches you, or a bouncing ball knocks you off, you're out. The last duck standing wins. Start with free Practice matches to get the hang of it.

💵 Betting & winning

Hit Cash Match and pick a stake: 5, 10, 50, or 100 credits (1 credit = $1). Everyone's stake goes into the pot, and the winner takes the whole pot minus a 10% fee. Practice matches are always free.

💰 Deposits & cashing out

Add credits with Bitcoin or Litecoin using the Deposit box below. Cashing out is automatic — enter your BTC/LTC address and amount under Withdraw and it's sent straight to your wallet. Minimum cash-out is 10 credits.
